Dog Names of Pittsburgh


.....


By: Sarah McIntosh

Picking a name for anything is probably one of the hardest things to do. You have that stress on your shoulders of picking that perfect name that will stick around for the rest of that things life.

Picking a name for your new dog is just as hard. You either have a named picked out already and you are sticking with that name. Or you have no idea and want to wait to get home with that special friend to see what their personailty is like.
Either way it's all stressful.

Now if you want to add more stress to your life with picking a name - your someone who wants to give your dog a unpopular name, so they can be unique and extra special at the dog. Have you ever been at a dog park and hear a common name like Buddy being called and you just see a sworm of dogs that have the name Buddy come running your way. I have - and as cool as it looks, trying to just get your dogs attention is nearly impossible.

So I wanted to see what the top 20 dog names were in Pittsburgh. Just like I thought they are all pretty basic names:

  • Abby
  • Angel
  • Bailey
  • Bella
  • Buddy
  • Charlie
  • Chole
  • Cooper
  • Daisy
  • Duke
  • Gracie
  • Harley
  • Lily
  • Lola
  • Lucy
  • Maggie
  • Max
  • Molly
  • Penny
  • Sadie

Of those top 20 names I have found out the top 5 and just how many dogs are registered (life time registrations ) with those names.

  1. Bella - 369
  2. Lucy - 257
  3. Molly - 242
  4. Bailey - 237
  5. Sadie - 225
